About Us

Welcome to Ruby's.ie - the online bookstore Ballybunion, Ireland.

We have a good selection of new and used/secondhand books available on our site

We offer free delivery on orders over €15 otherwise €3 postage applies within Ireland.

Our bookshop is solely online but based in Ballybunion, Ireland and orders are processed Monday to Saturday, 09:00 - 17:00 and shipped within 2 days of order being placed
(please allow 3 days for orders placed on sundays)

Ruby's online bookshop is a new business and hopes to bring joy to all you book lovers.

On Rubys.ie you’ll find a good variety of books from everything from the latest releases and bestsellers, to secondhand paperbacks. There are books in every genre such as children’s books, cookbooks, history, fiction, non-fiction and much more.

Enjoy using Rubys.ie and please feel free to contact us by email anytime with any book related query and recomendations, we'd be delighted to help.
